History

Built in 1876-77 by Cavaillé-Coll for St. Ferdinand et Thérèse de l'enfant de Jésu, Paris France. This instrument was modified only by Cavaillé-Coll, and no later than 1892. For more information visit www.organartmedia.com/CCMainz-Intro.html.
