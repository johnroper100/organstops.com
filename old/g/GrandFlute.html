Grand Flute English

Mentioned only in passing by Audsley[1] in a list of stop names having the word �Grand�, with no description. We can only assume that this name indicates some sort of flute stop of large scale and loud tone.

Examples

Grand Flute 8' (2 ranks), Swell; Grand Flute 8', Great; John Wanamaker Store, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA. This is the only known example of this name.
