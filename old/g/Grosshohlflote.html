Grosshohlflöt[e] German

A Hohlflöte of 16' pitch. Adlung reports that this stop �is very resonant in the bass�. In The Art of Organ-Building Audsley lists Grosshohlflöte as being synonymous with Hohlflötenbass, but lists them separately in his later work Organ-Stops and their Artistic Registration.
