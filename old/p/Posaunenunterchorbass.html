Posaunenbass Unterchormass German
Posaunenunterchorbass German

These names indicate a Posaune stop that appears at 16' in the pedal. The suffix bass indicates a stop that belongs to the pedal, and the term unterchormass indicates 16' pitch (see Chormassprincipal).
