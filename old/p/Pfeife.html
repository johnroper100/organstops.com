Pfeife German

While Audsley lists Pfeife as a synonym for Fife, he also lists it separately, calling it �in the generality of examples, practically identical with the stop named Fife�. While the stop name Pfeife is rarely found alone, it is a very common suffix for German stop names, often seemingly synonymous with the suffix Flöte. Williams states that it is, in most cases, interchangeable with Flöte. The word means �pipe� or �fife�.
