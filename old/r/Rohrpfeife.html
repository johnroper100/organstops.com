Rohrpfeife German

A variety of Rohrflöte mentioned only by Maclean, who gives it a narrow scale and long, wide chimneys, as opposed to the wide scale and short, narrow chimneys of the Rohrflöte.

Examples

Osiris contains around twenty examples: eight each at 4' and 2' pitch, and four at 8'. All are from the 20th century.

Rohrpfeife 4', Pedal; Martin Luther Gedaechtniskirche, Berlin, Germany; Walcker 1935.
