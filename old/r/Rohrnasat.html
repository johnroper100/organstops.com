 Nasard en Cheminée French

A Rohrflöte of 2-2/3' pitch. The name Rohrquinte has also been used as a synonym for Rohrflötenquinte. The name Nasard en Cheminée appears only in a sample stoplist in Dom Bedos. Nasards of that period commonly were made with chimneyed basses and conical trebles (see Nasard à Fuseau).

Examples

No examples of Nasard en Cheminée are known. Contributions welcome.

Rohrnasat 2-2/3', Schwellwerk; Concert Hall, Frederick Chopin Music Academy, Warsaw, Poland; Walcker 1996.

Rohrnasat 2-2/3', Schwellwerk; Sejong Cultural Center, Seoul, Korea; Schuke 1978.

Rohrnasat 2-2/3', Swell; Walla Walla College Church (Seventh-Day Adventist), Walla Walla, Washington, USA; Casavant 1962.
