Holzkrummhorn German

Not mentioned in the literature, this stop is presumably a Krummhorn with wooden resonators.

Examples

All known examples are listed below.

Holzkrummhorn 8', Oberwerk; Dom, Würzburg, Germany; Klais 1968. Mahogany.

Holzkrummhorn 8', Unterwerk; Münster, Bonn, Germany; Klais 1982.

Holzkrummhorn 8, Brustwerk; United Church of Christ Congregational, Ames, Iowa, USA; Kney 1972.
