Holzdulzian German

This stop is not mentioned in the literature; we assume it to be a form of Dulzian with wooden (holz) resonators.

Examples

Seven examples have been found in Osiris [1], all at 16' pitch, including the following:
 

Holzdulzian 16', Swell; St. John Lutheran Church, Knoxville, Tennesee, USA; Berghaus Organ Company, Bellwood Illinois, USA, 1992.

Holzdulzian 16' Oberwerk; Klosterkirche, Huysburg, Denmark; VEB Orgelbau Eule, Bautzen, 1983.

Holzdulzian 16', Oberwerk; Schauspielhauses, Berlin, Germany; Jehmlich.
