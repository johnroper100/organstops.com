M. P. Möller opus 8168, 1951; Fabry, Inc. 2006

These sound clips are a generous gift from Mr. John Gouwens, who performed and recorded them. Mr. Gouwens serves as Organist and Carilloneur of the Culver Academies. The sound clips were recorded using a Schoeps MK 21 microphone feeding a Zoom H4 digital recorder, recording directly to 160 bit mp3 format.

For more information on this organ, see http://faculty.culver.org/~gouwenj/organcarillon.asp
For general information about sound clips, see the Sound Files appendix.

A tour of the celestes, used in the following order: Swell Viole de Gambe and Viole Celeste; Swell Salicional and Voix Celeste; Choir Erzähler, Erzähler Celeste, 4' Coupler; Choir Viola and Viola Celeste; Swell Vox Humana, Viole de Gambe, Viole Celeste, and Tremolo, with Vox Humana, shutters closed, then open, then closed.

A demo of numerous solo flutes and reeds. The following solo stops are used in the following order: Oboe w/ Tremolo, Cor d'amour w/ Tremolo, Piccolo (Zauberflöte), Doppelflöte, English Horn w/Tremolo, Clarinet, Flûte Triangulaire w/ Tremolo.
