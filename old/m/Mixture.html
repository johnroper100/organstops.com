These are generic names for compound stops containing mutations, usually with breaks. When the name Mixture appears in a specification, it is usually some type of Fourniture.

See also mutations.

Examples

The stop-name Mixture is extremely common. No examples are known of the names Locatie, Locatio, Location, Lokatio, Micxtuere, Miscella, Positien, Position, Regula Mixta, or Starkwerk. Contributions welcome.

Positie, Werk; Abbey, Averbode, Belgium; Willem 1517.

Positie III, Manuaal; St. Quintens, Leuven, Belgium; Verrijt 1522.

Mixtuer IV-V 1-1/3', Positiv; Cathedral, Cologne, Germany; Klais 1947. This is the only known example of this spelling.
