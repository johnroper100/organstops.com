Metal[l]gedackt (German)
Metal[l]gedeckt (German)

A Gedeckt made of metal. See also Stopped Metallic, Holzgedeckt.

Examples

Osiris contains 20 examples, all at 8' pitch except for one each at 4' and 2' pitch. All of them date from the second half of the 20th century. The most common spelling is Metallgedackt.

Metal Gedeckt 8', Pedal; Central Baptist Church, Miami, Florida, USA; Schlicker 1974.

Metallgedackt 8', Schwellwerk; Dom, Würtzburg, Germany; Klais 1968.
