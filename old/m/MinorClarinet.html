Minor Clarinet English

Mentioned only by Irwin, who says: �Major Clarinet and Minor Clarinet have somewhat different meanings [from Solo Clarinet], not always implying the solo tonality.�

This stop would only appear in an organ which contained another Clarinet of more prominent tone.
