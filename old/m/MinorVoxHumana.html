Minor Vox Humana (unknown)

Mentioned only by Irwin, who says:

In the larger theater organs of from forty to eighty ranks, there may be as many as three Vox Humanas, typically called Minor Vox Humana (Accompanimental Organ), Major Vox Humana (Orchestral Organ), and Solo Vox Humana (Solo Organ).
