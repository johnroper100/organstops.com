Sanftgedackt German
Sanftgedeckt German
Sanftflöte German

A stopped wooden or metal flute of 8' or 4' pitch and soft tone, similar to the Lieblich Gedeckt, Echo Gedeckt, or Stillgedeckt.

There are other meanings for the name Sanftflöte.

Variants

Sanftbass - 16'

Examples

No examples of Sanftgedeckt are known. Contributions welcome.
