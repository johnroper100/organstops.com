Sanftflöte German

Audsley and Irwin equate this name with Sanftgedeckt. Locher considers it synonymous with Wienerflöte and Zartflöte. According to Wedgwood, it is synonymous with Vienna Flute, Flauto Amabile, and Lieblichflöte.

Examples

Only two examples of Sanftflöte are known.

Sanfte Flöte 4', Schwellwerk; St. Jakob, Prague, Czechoslovakia; Rieger-Kloss 1982.

Sanftflöte 4', Hauptwerk; Luitpoldhalle, Nürnberg, Germany; Walcker 1936 (destroyed).
