Spitz Nazard German
Spitz Nasat German

Not mentioned in the literature, these names presumably denote a Spitzflöte of 2-2/3' pitch.

Compare with Gemsquinte; see also Cylinderquint, Quint, Julaquinte, Spitzquinte.

Examples

All known examples are given below.

Spitz Nasat 2-2/3', Swell; Christ Church Cathedral, New Orleans, Louisiana, USA; Goulding & Wood 1994.

Spitz Nazard 2-2/3', Ghospel; First Congregational Church, Los Angeles, California, USA.
