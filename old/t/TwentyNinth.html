Twenty-Ninth English
Vigesima Nona Italian
Vigessima-nona Latin

Very rarely an independent stop, this rank is most often found as part of a Mixture. It sounds the 16th harmonic, at 1/2' for the 8' harmonic series, or 1' for the 16' harmonic series. It is a unison-sounding rank.

See also Campana, mutations.

Examples

Osiris contains eight examples of Vigesima Nona, two at 1/2' pitch, three at 1' pitch, and the rest of unknown pitch.
