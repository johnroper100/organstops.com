Tintinabulum (unknown)

A mixture stop of unknown composition.

Examples

Only two examples are known:

Tintinabulum III, Rückpositiv; Basilika, Steinfeld, Germany; König 1727, Klais 1934, Weimbs 1981.

Tintinabulum II 1', Schwell-Bombardwerk; Hauptorgel, Cathedral, Passau, Germany; Steinmeyer 1924-28.
