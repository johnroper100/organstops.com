Terzianscharf German?

Mentioned only by Irwin in a list of mixture stops under the heading �Color Due to Stop-Pitches�, with no definition. From the name we may infer that it is a cross between a Terzian and a Scharf.

See Sept-Terzianscharf.

Composition

From the Liebfrauendom (see below):

c'''8 12 15 17
f#'' 12 15 17 19
c'' 15 17 19 22
f#' 15 19 22 24
c' 19 22 24 26
f# 19 22 26
c 22 26 29
F# 26 29 33
C 29 33 36

Examples

Terzianscharf III-IV, Schwellwerk; Liebfrauendom, Munich, Germany; Zeilhuber 1957. This is the only known example.
