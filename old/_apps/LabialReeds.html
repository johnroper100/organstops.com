The following is a list of labial (flue) stops intended to produce the sound of reed stops, or to imitate orchestral tones which are usually approximated by reed stops. Some of these stops were developed in an attempt to find better imitations of orchestral instruments. Others were devised in order to stay in tune with the rest of the flue stops in an organ.
