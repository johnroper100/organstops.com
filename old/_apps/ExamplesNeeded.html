  • The exact name and pitch of the stop.
  • The name of the division in which it appears.
  • The name of the church or institution in which the organ resides.
  • The town or city, state or province, and country where the organ is located.
  • The name of the builder who made the stop, and the year it was made.
  • Whether or not the stop still exists.
  • Any information you may have about the construction of the stop.
Please note that in many cases, exact spelling is important.
