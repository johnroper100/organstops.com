Zaubergedeckt German

Listed only by Irwin, who says:

A softer form of the Zauberflöte, at 8' or 4' on the manuals. The mouths of this Flute have a higher cut-up and the pipes are of somewhat smaller scale than the Zauberflöte, making the timbre less well-rounded, pervading, and clear.
See Gedeckt.
