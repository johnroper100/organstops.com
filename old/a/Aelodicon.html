Aelodicon Greek
Aeolodicon Greek
Alodicon Greek

A soft-toned free reed stop of 16' pitch, a variant of the Aeoline Reed. According to Audsley, the name was used for a free-reed keyboard instrument, the precursor to the harmonium.

See Aeolina, Aeoline, Clavaeolina, Claväoline.

Examples

Aeolodicon 16', Brustwerk; Domes St. Maria (cathedral), Riga, Latvia; Walcker 1883 (restored 1983).
