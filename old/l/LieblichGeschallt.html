Lieblichgeschallt German

Wedgwood lists this stop with the following description:

LIEBLICH GESCHALLT - (Ger.) Schallen = to echo. 8 ft. Invented by Mr. Hope-Jones.
A very small scaled Echo Lieblichgedeckt. CC, 2 1/2 in.; mid. C, 7/8 in. Parkgate School, Cheshire (Hope-Jones). Kinnoul Church, Perth, N.B.
