Lieblich Quint German
Lieblichquinte German

Listed only by Audsley, who says:

The covered labial stop, of 5-1/3 ft. pitch, belonging to the Lieblichgedeckt family; the pipes of which are formed in all respects similar to those of the Lieblichgedeckt.

See Quint.

Examples

Lieblich Quint 5-1/3', Great; John Ledwon Residence, Agoura, California, USA; Wurlitzer. This is the only known example.
