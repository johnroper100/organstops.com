Nasard Harmonique French
Nazard Harmonique French

A Flûte Harmonique of 2-2/3' pitch. Rare.

Examples

All known examples are listed below.

Nasard harmonique 2-2/3', Recit expressif; Salle Olivier Messiaen, Radio France, Paris, France; Danion-Gonzalez, 1966, 1991.

Nasard harmonique 2-2/3', Solo; Eglise Notre-Dame-des-Graces, Woulwé St. Pierre, Belgium; Kleuker 1981.

Nasard harmonique 2-2/3', Grand Choeur; Tonhalle, Zuerich, Switzerland; Kleuker & Steinmeyer 1988.

Nazard harmonique 2-2/3', Solo; Eglise St-Vincent, Roquevaire, France; Birouste 1997.

Nazard harmonique 2-2/3', Recit; Broadway Baptist Church, Fort Worth, Texas, USA; Casavant 1996.

Nazard harmonique 2-2/3', Solo; St. Eustache, Paris, France; Dunand 1984 (removed).
