Concert Violin English

This stop is mentioned only by Strony, who writes:

This is a stop that was included in many Robert Morton theatre organs. Morton is well known for their especially potent String ranks. Unfortunately, some examples are nearly as loud as English Post Horns. Some are even louder!

Strony does not specify how this stop differs from other strings such as the Violin.
