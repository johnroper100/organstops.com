Clarín Fuerte Spanish

A Spanish Trumpet stop of strong (fuerte) tone. Grove gives it flared tin resonators.

See Clarín.

Examples

Clarin Fuerte 8', Second Manual, left hand; Toledo Cathedral, Toledo, Spain; Valentin and Jose Verdalonga 1798.

Clarin Fuerte 4', Trompeteria; Turku Cathedral, Finland; Veikko Virtanen 1980.
