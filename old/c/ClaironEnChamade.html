Clairon en Chamade French

A Clairon which is mounted en chamade, which may or may not be externally visible; a 4' Trompette en Chamade. For more information, see Chamade.

Examples

Osiris contains a dozen examples, the oldest of which are listed below.

Clairon en Chamade 4', Solo Expressif; Albert Hall, Sheffield, England; Cavaillé-Coll 1873 (destroyed).

Clairon en Chamade 4', Grand Orgue; St. Sernin, Toulouse, France; Cavaillé-Coll 1889.

Clairon en Chamade 4', Grand-Orgue; Church of St-Ouen, Rouen, France; Cavaillé-Coll 1890.
