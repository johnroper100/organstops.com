Clarín de Compaña Spanish

Grove and Williams describe Clarín de Batalla and Clarín de Compaña only as military Trumpet stops en chamade. Maclean describes Clarín de Campaña only as an 8' military or field trumpet. Audsley mentions Clarin de Batalla only as a variant of Clarin, with no description.

Examples

No examples of Clarín de Compaña are known. Contributions welcome.
