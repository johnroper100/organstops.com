Clarines Spanish

Mentioned only by Grove and Williams, who say: �usually ... a 2' reed of soft Trumpet tone, sometimes a bass-half stop only.� The known examples contradict this definition (see below); the only 2' Clarines we know of are named Clarines en Quincena.
