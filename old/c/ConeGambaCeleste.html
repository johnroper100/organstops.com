Cone Gamba Céleste English?

A two-rank Céleste based on the Cone Gamba, tuned either normal/sharp or normal/flat.

Examples

We know of only one example:

Cone Gamba Celeste 8', Swell; Conventional Hall, Atlantic City, New Jersey, USA; Midmer-Losh 1929-32.
