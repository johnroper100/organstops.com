Echo Gemshorn English

Listed only by Irwin, who says:

A very soft Foundation stop of 8' or 4' on the manuals, yielding a delicate, clear, somewhat silvery Gemshorn tone, but much reduced in volume. This stop enters into combination with any other flue stop, and is an ideal soft accompaniment, perhaps for an Echo Oboe or Orchestral Flute stop. It is formed like the bigger Gemshorn.
