Ethereal Violin Celeste English

Listed only by Irwin, who says:

An undulating String stop of 8' manual pitch, composed of two, three, or more ranks of Ethereal Violin pipes. This tone is attractive because it is soft in contrast to its unusual brilliance. Its shimmering, delicate, lustrous effect is one of the most beautiful obtainable among the Strings of the organ.
See Celeste.
