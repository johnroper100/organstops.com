Echo Fifteenth English

Mentioned only by Bonavia-Hunt, who says:

The 2ft. flute is not the best of tone builders, it being only successful in combination with the other flutes of its division, hence the salicetina or echo fifteenth is taking its place in the modern echo and choir divisions as being more generally suitable.
