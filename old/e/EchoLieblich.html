Echo Lieblich German

Mentioned only by Skinner, who says: �the name given to the Swell Bourdon when it is duplexed to the Pedal organ for a light pedal voice.� Compare with Lieblich Gedeckt.

Examples

Osiris contains eleven examples, most by Skinner, all at 16' pitch, and all but two consistent with Skinner's description.

Echo Lieblich 16', Pedal; Smith Hall, Wells College, Aurora, New York, USA; Skinner 1906. This is the oldest known example.
