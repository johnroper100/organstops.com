Basskornett German?

This stop is mentioned in the literature only by Williams, who describes it as a 2' pedal reed, essentially a synonym for Zink. However, the only known example is a mixture, possibly synonymous with Basszink.

Examples

Basskornett IV, Pedal; Stadtkirche, Weimar, Germany; Walcker 1908.
